# Code of conduct

This applies to the workshop itself and to this repository's community spaces
(issues, pull requests and discussions).

## Our standard

We are a community of researchers practising **critical, respectful enquiry**. We
expect everyone to:

- Treat others with **courtesy and good faith**, especially in disagreement.
- **Welcome scepticism and dissent** about tools and ideas — challenging a claim is
  encouraged; demeaning a person is not.
- Be **considerate** of differing experience levels; no question about a tool is too
  basic.
- Respect **privacy and confidentiality** — do not share others' data, words or work
  without consent, online or in the room.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, demeaning comments, sustained
disruption and sharing others' personal or confidential information without
consent.

## Scope

The workshop room, breakout groups, the shared notes and any repository space
connected to this project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, contact the facilitator at
**pablo.bernabeu@education.ox.ac.uk**. Reports will be handled discreetly and in line with institutional
policy. During the session, you may raise a concern with the facilitator directly
and in confidence.

This is a lightweight, workshop-specific code and is not a substitute for your
institution's own policy, which continues to apply.
